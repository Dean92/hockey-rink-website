Public Skate

Lace up and hit the ice! Open skating sessions for all ages and skill levels throughout the week.

Schedule

Sessions run Monday–Friday 12:00 PM–2:00 PM and Saturday–Sunday 1:00 PM–3:00 PM and 4:00 PM–6:00 PM. Holiday hours may vary.

Pricing

Adults (18+): $8 per session. Youth (under 18): $6 per session. Skate rentals available for $4. Season passes available for frequent skaters.

What to Know

Helmets are recommended for all skaters and required for children under 12. No hockey sticks or pucks during public sessions. Lockers available on site.
