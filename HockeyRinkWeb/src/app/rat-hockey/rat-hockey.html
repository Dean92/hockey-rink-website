Rat Hockey

Drop-in hockey at its finest. No commitments, no teams — just show up and play with whoever's on the ice.

Drop-In Schedule

Monday, Wednesday & Friday: 10:00 PM–Midnight. Sunday afternoons: 3:00 PM–5:00 PM. No pre-registration needed — first come, first on the ice.

Pricing

$12 per session. Pay at the door — cash or card accepted. Monthly unlimited pass available for $90. Goalies skate free to encourage balanced teams.

House Rules

Full equipment required. Slapshots allowed at late-night sessions only. Teams are chosen on arrival. Good sportsmanship is expected and enforced.
