Stick & Puck

Affordable open-ice time to sharpen your skills, work on your shot, and skate at your own pace.

Schedule

Tuesday & Thursday 6:00 AM–7:30 AM. Saturday 7:00 AM–8:30 AM. Additional sessions added during peak season. Check back for updates.

Pricing

$10 per session per skater. No pre-registration required — just show up and pay at the door. Punch cards (10 sessions) available for $85.

Requirements

Full hockey equipment required: helmet with cage, gloves, shin guards, and skates. Sticks and pucks welcome. Sessions are unsupervised open-ice time.
