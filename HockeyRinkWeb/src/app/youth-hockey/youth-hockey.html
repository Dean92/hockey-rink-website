Youth Hockey

Helping young players develop skills, teamwork, and a lifelong love of the game in a fun and safe environment.

Age Groups

Programs available for Mite (8U), Squirt (10U), Peewee (12U), Bantam (14U), and Midget (18U) age groups. All skill levels welcome.

Season & Schedule

Main season runs October through March. Two practices and one game per week. Weekend tournament opportunities available for competitive teams.

Fees & Equipment

Registration fees start at $650 per player per season, including jersey. Full protective equipment required. Loaner equipment available for first-year players.
